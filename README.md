## Project Overview
The purpose of this project is to analyze the sales dataset to uncover key business insights, understand customer purchasing behavior, monitor profitability, and provide data-driven recommendations that can help improve sales performance, inventory planning, and operational efficiency.

## Objectives
- Which countries contribute the highest sales?
- Profit margin comparison per country.
- Identify the sales and profit over the trend of time.
- Which age group is responsible for the most sales and profit.
- Determine which gender drives the most sales.

## Dataset Description
- Source : Kaggle
- Over 100,000+ rows and 18 columns.
- Important columns (Date, Category, Sub-Category, Product Name, Order Quantity, Revenue, etc.).

## Data Cleaning and Preparation
- Converted dataset into a table.
- Created pivot tables from the table.
- Derived additional field like profit margin.
- Built a dashboard of pivot charts from the pivot tables.

## Exploratory Data Analysis
- Used Power Query and Excel
- Created calculated fields.
- Standardized data formats.

## Insights
- United States dominates total sales volume.
- Canada, despite having lower total sales than Australia, delivers the highest profit margin (46.8%), indicating excellent pricing, cost control, or customer targeting efficiency.
- 2015 had peak sales with a decline in 2016.
- Top 2 markets (United Kingdom, Canada) contribute the majority of overall profits.

## Recommendations
- Focus on best-selling and high-profit products.
- Maintain strong supply levels, launch loyalty programs, promote high-margin accessories.
- Identify countries where profit margin < 20% and review cost/discount structure.
- Marketing investment could boost demand (e.g., France).
